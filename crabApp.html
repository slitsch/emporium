How to make crab-apple vodka or gin

You will need:
  1. A glass gallon jug with well fitting lid
  2. 8 cups of washed and halved crab-apples
  3. Remove any maggots
  4. 1.75 lt vodka or gin
  5. That's right, the biggest bottle
  6. It's OK to use the cheap stuff
  7. 1 cup of icing sugar

Mix everything together in the bottle.
Turn and shake every day for 16 days.
